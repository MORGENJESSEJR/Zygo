# Zygo Product Brief

## Product

- Name: Zygo
- Category: Mobility and local haulage platform
- Launch market: Harare, Zimbabwe first

## Target User

Harare informal traders who need same-day transport for themselves, their goods, or both.

## Core Problem

Booking the right vehicle for a person, luggage, or goods takes too much time and uncertainty when the transport need changes during the day.

## Differentiator

Zygo lets one user book rides or hire the right vehicle type in one place and pay with familiar methods including EcoCash.

## Design Intent

- Grounded: warm surfaces, clear hierarchy, honest UI
- Decisive: one dominant CTA, strong selected states, minimal drift
- Reassuring: verified-driver cues, payment visibility, predictable layout
- Adaptive: start from the task, then reveal the vehicle fit
